The Author

Dr. Grace Talatu Marshall

Dr. Grace Talatu Marshall draws on a lifetime of lessons in resilience, faith, and family to share a story of how a father's unwavering belief shaped the woman she would become.

Raised across continents and through countless transitions, she carried forward the values instilled in her — eventually pursuing a doctorate inspired by her father's example. Raised Without Limits is her invitation to readers everywhere: to embrace where you come from, walk boldly in your purpose, and pursue your dreams without limits.
